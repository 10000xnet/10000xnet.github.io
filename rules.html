📜 10000x.net Server Rules 📜

Keep it fun, keep it fair. If you’re unsure whether something is allowed — ask in Discord.
✅ PvP Server 🚫 No Exploits 🏠 Base Limits 💥 Raid Rules
👮‍♂️ Admins reserve the right to kick/ban for any toxic or disruptive behavior not covered below.
🚫 General Rules
These are the core “don’t ruin the server” rules.
  • 🎭 No Drama. If you stir up drama or act drama-hungry, you’ll be kicked/banned. No questions asked.
  • 🧠 No Cheating / Exploits. No white-walling, shooting through walls on login, or similar exploits.
  • 👶 No Spawn/Freshie Camping. Don’t excessively camp spawns or boost killstreaks.
  • 🏚️ No Building on Loot Shacks. Don’t build in/on loot shacks or place explosives (e.g., land mines) inside them.
  • 🛣️ No explosives or building on roads. Keep roads playable.
  • 🪵 No Log Walls. Walls built out of logs are not permitted.
  • 🚪 Entry Builds. Gates and entryways must be placed outside of existing structures. No glitching half a structure into a wall.
  • 🗼 Watchtower Height Limit. Maximum watchtower height is 3 floors.
  • 🫡 Respect Others. No doxing, personal/family insults, etc. Keep beefs in-game only.
  • 🎭 No Impersonation. Don’t impersonate other players or create similar gamertags to troll or cause drama.
  • 🚷 No running with banned players. Don’t run with banned players or let them use your base / faction base.
  • 🧯 Don’t Be Annoying. If it’s clearly toxic/disruptive, it’s a problem.
💥 Raiding Rules
Raid hard — but don’t abuse mechanics or wipe bases for no reason.
  • 🚪 No Base Logging. Don’t log out in a base that isnt yours/your factions.
  • 🧨 No Griefing. Destroying more than 50% of an enemy base is not allowed. This includes removing complete watchtowers/gates/etc.
  • 🐛 No Exploits. Do not use exploits (building/vehicles/tents, etc.) to enter bases.
  • 🏗️ No Building in Enemy Bases. Don’t place structures inside enemy bases during/after a raid.
  • ⏱️ Post-Raid Occupancy. After a successful raid, your team may occupy the enemy base for up to 1 full reset (after all enemies are killed or the raid is complete). After that, you must leave.
  • 🚩 Flag Rules. Swapping/raising your own flag at an enemy base is OK. Do not leave the flagpole empty or remove the flagpole structure.
🏠 Base Owner Rules
Base rules are about fair raiding and preventing unraidable choke points.
  • 🔢 Keypad/Accessible Entrance. All bases must have a keypad-accessible door or raidable entrance.
  • 🚫 No Blocking Entrances. Do not block main entrances with watchtowers or stacks of fence posts. Gates and walls are OK for extra protection.
  • 🚪 Main Entrance Limit. Maximum 10 walls/gates to block your main entrance.
  • 🗼 Watchtower Limit. Maximum 10 watchtowers total. Each watchtower may have up to 3 levels (floors).
  • 🧱 Non-entry Walls/Gates Limit. Maximum 25 non-entry gates/walls.
  • 💣 No Hoarding Explosives. Don’t stockpile excessive explosives.
